    ABOUT US

    Founded in 1986, our company is the first translation office of Kocaeli. With its team having the necessary educational background and vision, Çağ Çeviri assists its customers as a solution partner. We serve to our customers with the awareness that accurate, complete and timely delivery of translations is the primary prerequisite of quality in the field of translation. We utilize the means of communication enabling an effective flow of communication with our customers. The company policy of Çağ Çeviri is to correctly identify the translation needs of its customers and to offer them services in a way to exactly fulfill those needs.

    OUR SERVICES

    Notarized Translation, Commercial Translation, Technical Translation, Legal Translation, Medical Translation, Website Translation, Interpreting (Consecutive Translation), Transcription

    Notarized Translation

    All your corporate or personal documents requiring notarization for submission to the official authorities are translated by our certified translators.

    Commercial Translation

    Translation of all kinds of texts needed by commercial companies are translated by our expert translators.

    Technical Translation

    With our team of translators specialized in technical terminology, we offer technical translation services in different fields.

    Legal Translation

    It is the written translation service provided by translators experienced in national and international law.

    Medical Translation

    All kinds of medical documents are carefully translated by translators who are experienced in their field.

    Interpreting (Consecutive Translation)

    It is the translation service offered by the translator to enable communication between the parties, generally following the speech of the speaker.

    Transcription

    It is the service of listening to and writing down the voice records in Turkish or different languages.
